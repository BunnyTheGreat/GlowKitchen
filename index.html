Hi, I'm Aishani!

I love to go hiking, read, listen to music, but most importantly, I love to cook! I started this blog not only because of my passion for food, but because I ran into a problem: I wanted to find quick and easy recipes that I could eat, even when my eczema is flared. But how do I know what was safe? That is why I implemented my flare-friendly meter, where you can see if the ingredients in a recipe can aggrevate your skin even more on your bad days. I hope you enjoy my recipes as much as I love making them!
